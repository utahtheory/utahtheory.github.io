University of Utah Theory Reading Group

We are a student-led reading group at the Kahlert School of Computing, and every week we host a talk in theoretical computer science research from topics across the field. All are welcome!

Logistics

Time: Tuesdays, typically 12:30pm to 1:30pm, followed by (BYO) lunch!

Location: Theory group is usually at MEB 3485.

Contact: Eli Friedman (eli.friedman at utah.edu). Send questions, comments, or requests to speak.
